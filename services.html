Printing & Copying

Patrons logged into our WiFi can print to our Public Laser at the Circulation Desk. You are responsible for all pages that are printed from your device. The printer should appear as BP-50C26 (Circulation Desk). You may need to install the driver for the printer. Drivers for your device are available below. If you have any questions, a Librarian at the Circulation Desk will be able to assist you.

Price Guide

  • Prints are $0.25 per page, per side
  • There is no price difference for color or black & white copies

Faxing

Patrons can have their documents faxed at the Circulation Desk with the assistance of a Librarian. Patrons must provide the fax number for the organization they wish to send documents too. If you do not have the fax number, a Librarian
can assist you with obtaining the information.

Price Guide

  • $1.00 per page, per side
  • Cover letters are avaliable for an additional $1.00

Lamination

Patrons can have documents laminated with the assistance of a Librarian at the Circulation Desk.

Price Guide

  • $1.00 for full page
  • $0.50 for half a page

Scanning

Patrons who wish to scan documents can request a computer with a scanner or ask a Librarian at the Circulation
Desk to assist them with the process. There is no charge for scanning.
